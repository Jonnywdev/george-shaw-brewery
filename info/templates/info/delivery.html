Delivery

Shipping to the UK

Free shipping applies to all UK orders over Â£50, otherwise we will add a 10% delivery fee to your order at checkout. Orders to Scottish Islands, Channel Islands or the Isle of Man may require additional postage fees.
We offer 3-day delivery on all our products to customers in England, Scotland, Northern Ireland and Wales, but it's likely that your delivery may arrive much quicker than this. In fact, 98% of orders placed before 2pm are delivered to customers the next working day.
All of our orders are delivered by DHL. During December and other busy periods, please be aware that delivery times may be impacted.

Collection from the brewery

Collection from the brewery is only available to those age 18 or over. We may ask to see ID and reserve the right not to serve anyone we believe to be underage or intoxicated. We hold our collection days on the last Saturday of the month at Leigh Rufc. Sign up to our news letter or follow us on social media for more information.

Shipping to the EU & overseas

Unfortunately, we are not currently able to ship our products to customers outside of the UK.

Age restriction

You must be 18 or older to buy alcohol. By placing an order with us online you are confirming you are 18 or older. It is an offence to sell alcohol to anyone under the age of 18 in the UK.
If you are buying alcohol for someone else, the recipient must also be 18 or older.
Deliveries must be accepted by a person aged 18 or older. If our couriers are in any doubt about the age of the recipient on delivery, they will request photo ID. We reserve the right to cancel the delivery if the age and identity of the recipient is in doubt.

Orders

If we are unable to fulfil the whole of your order for any reason we will attempt to contact you via the contact email address provided on your order. We may as a result replace items with a similar value/size/type. In exceptional circumstances we reserve the right to hold off your order and contact you for further instruction on how you would like to proceed.

Faulty or damaged items

If there is a problem with your order or items are faulty, you must inform George Shaw Brewery within 30 days of receipt of the order to obtain a refund. To report a problem please contact us at info@georgeshawbrewery.com. We are unfortunately unable to offer refunds after this time period has elapsed, but we will do everything in our power to try to work something out.

If an attempted delivery is obviously damaged, you are within your rights to refuse the order, at which point it will be returned to us. If you choose to do this, please contact us at info@georgeshawbrewery.com and we will issue either a resend of the order, or a full refund, whichever is preferable.

We cannot accept responsibility for delivery failure if you provide an incorrect address and postal code or incorrect contact telephone number. Please check this information when placing your order.

This does not affect your statutory rights.

Taxes & other charges

For orders that are paid for in the UK, VAT is payable on all online shop orders regardless of the final destination country. We keep all international surcharges to a minimum where possible, but this charge is not something we have the ability to waiver.

These terms and conditions do not affect your statutory rights.
